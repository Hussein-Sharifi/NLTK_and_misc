Definitions:

Stylistics: a type of linguistic inquiry concerned with differences between categories.

Module: a collection of functions you can call in an interpreter. eg: nltk.corpus.brown
Package: a collection of modules: eg. nltk.corpus
Library: a collection of packages eg. nltk

Lexicon: a collections of words/phrases with associated linguistic information

word token vs word type: word tokens are each occurrence of every word. word types is the set of the vocabulary with no duplicates

normalizing text: for example using w.lower() to normalize words to lower case. A further step would be stripping of affixes, AKA stemming. Then turning it into a dictionary, AKA lemmatization
